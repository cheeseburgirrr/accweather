Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Accurate Weather Forecast Our. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information related to your use of our website.

Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect certain information from you:

  • Your geolocation data through your device's location access to provide you with accurate weather data based on your current location.
  • Non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information, collected through cookies and similar technologies for analytical purposes to enhance our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with accurate weather data based on your current location.
  • To analyze website usage patterns and improve our services and user experience.
  • To respond to your inquiries or customer service requests.
  • To personalize your experience on our website.
  • To send you occasional promotional materials or updates about our products and services, if you have provided explicit consent for such communications.

Location Access

We use your device's location access solely to provide you with accurate weather data based on your current location. Your geolocation data is not stored, and we do not track your location when you are not using our website.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to assist us in analyzing website usage and improving our services. These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your non-personal information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
